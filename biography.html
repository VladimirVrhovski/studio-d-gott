Biography

„The artist does not create merely for his own satisfaction, but wants his work validated by others. In fact, the creative process is not completed until the work has found an audience. In the end, work of art exist in order to be liked rather than to be debated.“
- H.W. Janson

Dejana Gott

Graduated from the High School for Industrial Design in Belgrade in 1991. First prize for painting "Petar Lubarda" in 1994. Graduated in painting at the Faculty of Fine Arts in Belgrade in 1998. First solo exhibition in the gallery of the Art Club Energoprojekt, Belgrade in 1999. The last exhibition in 2024 in the Student Center of Pula. I have been working as a teacher in art schools since 2000. Member of ULUS (Union of Fine Artists of Serbia) and HDLU of Istria (Croatian Society of Fine Arts of Istria).
